## Step 1: Log Into Square

1. Go to [squareup.com](https://squareup.com)
2. Click **Sign In** in the top right
3. Log in with your Square account (the same one you use for in-person sales)

---

## Step 2: Make Sure Online Payments Are Enabled

1. Once logged in, click the **Settings** gear icon (‚öôÔ∏è)
2. Look for **Business** or **Account & Settings**
3. Find **Online Payments** or **eCommerce**
4. Make sure it shows as **Enabled**

*If it's not enabled, there should be a button to turn it on ‚Äî it's usually instant since your account is already verified.*

## Step 3: Go to the Square Developer Dashboard

This is where we get the "keys" that connect your website to Square.

1. Open a new tab and go to: **[developer.squareup.com](https://developer.squareup.com)**
2. Click **Sign In** (use the same Square account)
3. You'll see the Developer Dashboard

## Step 4: Create an Application

1. Click the **"+"** button or **"Create Application"**
2. Name it: **Creativity by Lily Website**
3. Click **Save** or **Create**
